Popcorn Light Sculpture - Edition 5C04

£120.00

A beautiful light sculpture created using a handcrafted algorithm. Each edition of this sculpture is its own unique permutation of the algorithm. The base is handmade ceramic and encases an energy efficient, USB powered LED light. The shade is 3D printed in recycled plastic and when pressed gently from above, turns the light on and off with a satisfying click.

Materials: Recycled plastic, ceramic, LED light

Light source: Built-in 1w LED light. Fits a standard USB-A socket (wall adapter not included)

Light colour: Warm white (2700 Kelvin)

Dimensions: 50mm x 50mm x 125mm, cable length 2m

Made in London, UK.
